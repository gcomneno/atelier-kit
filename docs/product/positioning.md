# Atelier-Kit positioning

Atelier-Kit is a lightweight configurable showcase kit for makers, artists, artisans and small creative activities.

It helps creators present handmade items through a clean catalog, item detail pages and optional Signal Clouds.

It is designed for people who need a simple online showcase, not a full e-commerce system.

## What it is

- a configurable showcase template;
- a catalog for handmade creations;
- a small SvelteKit product;
- a deployable starter kit;
- a possible setup/customization service.

## What it is not

- Shopify;
- Etsy;
- WordPress;
- a marketplace;
- a SaaS platform;
- a CRM;
- a social network;
- a public commenting system.

## Core promise

Configure the site, add items and images, deploy the showcase.

No cart. No accounts. No contact-form swamp.
