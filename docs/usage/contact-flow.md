# No-textarea contact flow

Atelier-Kit can support a lightweight contact flow without adding a contact form.

The idea is simple:

1. a visitor opens an item page;
2. the visitor selects one or more Signal Cloud answers;
3. Atelier-Kit generates a Visitor Brief;
4. the visitor copies the brief;
5. the visitor pastes it into an external contact channel.

This keeps the showcase privacy-friendly and avoids form spam.

## Example Visitor Brief

```text
I am interested in "Example Creation".

My impressions:
- This creation feels...: bold
- I would imagine it...: on a shelf

Item page: https://example.com/items/example-item
```

## Why no textarea?

A textarea inside the site usually implies more product scope:

- form handling;
- spam protection;
- validation;
- privacy policy considerations;
- possible email delivery problems;
- server-side storage or third-party services.

Visitor Brief avoids that.

The visitor writes through their own chosen channel.

## Possible contact page

A future contact page can stay simple and file-based:

- email link;
- Instagram link;
- WhatsApp link;
- Signal link;
- studio/location note;
- instruction to paste the copied Visitor Brief.

No message box is required.
